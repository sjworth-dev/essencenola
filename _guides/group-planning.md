## Step 1: Know the Festival

### Essence Fest 2026

**Dates:** July 2-5, 2026 (Thursday-Sunday)

**Two Main Components:**

| Venue | What | When |
|-------|------|------|
| Superdome | Nightly concerts | Fri, Sat, Sun nights (7pm-late) |
| Convention Center | Empowerment seminars, marketplace, daytime events | Thurs-Sun (10am-6pm) |

### The Daily Structure

**Daytime (Convention Center):**
- Free admission
- Empowerment Experience: panels, speakers, wellness
- Marketplace: Black-owned vendors
- Beauty & style experiences
- Meet and greets

**Nighttime (Superdome):**
- Ticketed concerts
- Major R&B, hip-hop, gospel headliners
- Three nights of performances
- Each night is different artists

## Step 2: Tickets Strategy

### Superdome Concert Tickets

| Type | Approx. Price | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Single night | $150-250 | Pick your headliners |
| 3-night package | $400-600 | All three concert nights |
| VIP/Premium | $800+ | Better seating, perks |

**Buy early.** Essence sells out. Prices increase as the date approaches.

### Convention Center

**Free.** Register online for the Empowerment Experience but no ticket needed.

### Group Ticket Buying

**Option 1:** Everyone buys their own
- Simple
- People can choose their nights
- May end up in different sections

**Option 2:** One person buys all tickets together
- Same section, sit together
- Requires upfront coordination
- Collect money first

**Recommendation:** Buy as a group if you want to sit together. The Superdome is massiveâ€”being separated isn't ideal.

## Step 3: Accommodations

### Why Private Houses

During Essence weekend:

| Factor | Private House | Hotel |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| Space for 15 | One property | 7-8 rooms, scattered |
| Morning recovery | Pool, kitchen, space | Cramped room |
| Pre-concert prep | Full house to get ready | Hotel bathroom |
| Price per person | $150-300/night | $350-500/night |
| Vibes | Your own space | Hotel crowds |

### Book EARLY

Essence is one of the biggest events in New Orleans. Properties book 6-12 months out.

| Timing | Reality |
|--------|---------|
| 9-12 months before | Best selection, best prices |
| 6-9 months before | Good options remain |
| 3-6 months before | Slim pickings |
| Last minute | You'll pay double or more |

### Location Priorities

**Best for Essence:**
1. **CBD/Warehouse District** â€” Walking distance to Superdome and Convention Center
2. **French Quarter** â€” Close to everything, Uber short
3. **Marigny/Bywater** â€” Fun neighborhoods, 10-15 min to venues

**Avoid:** Anything requiring 30+ min transport to downtown.

### The Getting Ready Situation

With 10-15 people and limited bathrooms, you need a system:

**Bathroom Rotation:**
- Create time slots (45 min each)
- Start early (4-5pm for 9pm departure)
- Showers first, then makeup stations elsewhere

**Glam Setup:**
- Designate makeup/hair stations in bedrooms
- Bring ring lights and mirrors
- Create a playlist for getting ready

**The 2-Hour Rule:** Whatever time you think you need, add 2 hours.

## Step 8: Transportation

### Getting to New Orleans

**Louis Armstrong Airport (MSY):** 30-40 min from downtown

**From Airport:**
- Uber/Lyft: $30-45
- Uber XL (6 people): $40-55
- Coordinate arrivals to share rides

### Getting Around During Essence

**To Convention Center/Superdome:**
- Walk if you're in CBD (best)
- Uber (expect surge pricing at concert end)
- Rideshare after concerts is CHAOSâ€”be patient

**Pro Tips:**
- Leave concerts 15 min early to beat the Uber rush
- Set pickup points away from main exits
- Or walk back if you're close enough

**For Day Parties:**
- Uber is fine during daytime
- One person books all rides, Splitwise after

## What Can Go Wrong

### Someone Drops Out
- Deposits are non-refundable
- Remaining squad splits their share
- Emergencies get handled case by case

### Concert Section Issues
- If seated separately, meet at set times during breaks
- Know how to find each other in the Superdome
- Set a meeting point outside after

### Squad Drama
- Address issues privately, not in group chat
- Remember why you came
- Take breaks from each other if needed

### Overdoing It
- Pace yourselvesâ€”it's 4 days
- Hydrate constantly (New Orleans in July is HOT)
- It's okay to skip something and rest
